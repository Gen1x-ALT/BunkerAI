Bunker AI

Every new game is a new adventure

Bunker AI is a game where you're placed into Pang's shoes (or flippers), a penguin who's been trapped in a scientific base after all Artificial Intelligence on the planet went rogue and massacred all humans off the face of the earth, only leaving the animals.

Your mission is to escape the base and get the machines to notice and rescue you in time.
